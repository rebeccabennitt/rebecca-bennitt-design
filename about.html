About Me

Hi, I'm Rebecca

Communicator. People person. Problem Solver. Design & Coding enthusiast. Native Mississippian. Culture vulture. Lover of color & flowers. Dogs & writers, too. Pittsburgh Sports junkie. Secretly coastal. Slightly absurd.

I have a variety of interests -- art & design, literature & film, history, Pittsburgh sports teams. I like being busy, have an incredible work ethic and am a bit of a jack of all trades. In my previous lives, I have worked in a variety of fields -- radio news, event planning/floral design, retail and restaurant management, and most recently PR & administrative work in higher education. However, I was usually asked to help design, build or maintain several of my employers' websites. I managed to make things work, by using the few HTML/CSS tricks I had, working thru online tutorials and by copying and pasting together bits of code from stack overflow. I developed a real passion for design and development and I always wanted to understand more about user experience design and web development! I found Bloc and began working part time on the Design Track in August 2015.
